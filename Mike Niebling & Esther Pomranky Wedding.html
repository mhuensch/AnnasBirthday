Esther & Mike's romance timeline

Mike and Esther

7/12/2014

July 6, 2010

Esther and Mike Meet

Esther: Mike met Laura, one of my friends, on Match.com. Laura had told me quite a bit about Mike and her other Match.com prospects. I think I even saw his profile at one point. After a few dates, they decided they'd just be friends, and she invited Mike over to her apartment for a dinner party. I'd already made plans to have dinner at a Moroccan restaurant with a teacher friend, so I arrived quite a bit after everyone else. I remember seeing Mike for the first time and exclaiming (much to Laura's chagrin), "Oh, you're the tap dancer!"

July 15, 2010

Mike Starts Coming to Southport Salsa

Mike: I always thought partner dancing was something every gentleman should be able to do. So after trying out salsa and bumping my head on the ceiling that first night at Laura's, I was definitely down to start attending the Southport weekly... especially after Esther "taught" me how to club bachata on my first night!

October 4, 2010

Mike Starts Attending Dance Class

Esther: After a few months of dancing with us in my living room, Mike decided to give dance class a try. He picked up the footwork so quickly that he soon became my favorite dance partner.
