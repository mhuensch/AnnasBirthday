The Perfect Mirror

For years, Anna has been looking for the perfect mirror ...

Many of you may not know this, but Anna has been looking for the perfect mirror. Every time we go to a furniture store, thirft shop, or antinque shop; Anna looks over their selection of mirrors looking for the perfect mirror.

Size

It hads to be tall ...

The most important feature of the mirror is that it has to be tall. Very tall. Anna wants a mirror that relects everything from head to toe with plenty of room to spare.

Looks

It had to be French ...

In keeping with her love of things French, Anna wants a mirror that has the look of french style and culture. Additionaly, it is important that the mirror has a very ornate quality and is a quality piece that she can have and appriciate for the rest of her life.

Lifestyle

It had to fit her lifestyle ...

No, this is NOT just a gratuitous shot of Dexter to give you something cute to look at, it really is important that the mirror can be an every day part of Anna's life (just like Dexter).

The Gift

A few weeks ago, while shopping for Halloween decorations at the thrift store, Anna found the mirror of her dreams! It's a 19th century reproduction of an 18th century french mirror. Its in very good shape and stands over 7 feet tall.

The Goal
