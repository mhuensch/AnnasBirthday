We Found the Perfect Gift
for Anna's 25th Birthday!

The Story

Why This Gift?

It's really an obsession.

For years, Anna has been looking for the perfect mirror. Every time we go questing for new antiques, Anna won't let us leave without looking over their selection of mirrors.

The Right Size

Something Not too big, Not too small.

Actually it probably couldn't be too big. Anna's been looking for a piece of furniture to really make a statement. Something with immense stature to reflect everything from head to toe with plenty of room to spare.

The Right Look

Something Marie-Antoinette would covet.

In keeping with her love of all things French, Anna wants a mirror that embodies ornate French style and extravagance. Something you might go visit at Versailles. Something to remind her of her European adventures abroad.

The Right Quality

Something Anna will cherish for a lifetime.

No, this is NOT just a gratuitous shot of Dexter to give you something cute to look at, it really is important that the mirror can be an everyday part of Anna's life (just like Dexter). Anna is looking for a lasting piece of furniture that she can have and appreciate for the rest of her life.

The Gift

A few weeks ago, while shopping for Halloween decorations at our favorite antique store, Anna found the mirror of her dreams! It's a 19th century reproduction of an 18th century Rococo floor mirror. Its in very good shape and stands over 7 feet tall.

The Goal

Unfortunatly, the most we could talk the owner down to was $600.00 - which is still a lot of cash to cover in one purchace. So I suggested to Anna that we try and croud source it as a present for her birthday.
